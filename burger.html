Schnitzel Burger

Try our Schnitzel Burgerâ€”a delicious combination of crispy schnitzel, fresh toppings, and creamy sauces, all sandwiched between soft buns. It's a satisfying meal that's sure to please!

prep time

10 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Meat Dishes

instructions

step 1

Pound chicken or pork until 0,5cm thick. Season with salt and pepper.

step 2

Coat in flour, beaten eggs, and breadcrumbs. Refrigerate for 15 minutes.

step 3

Fry schnitzels in hot oil until golden brown and cooked through.

step 4

Spread sauce on buns. Add schnitzels, lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles.

step 5

Serve with your favorite sides and enjoy!

ingredients

For the schnitzel

4 boneless chicken breasts or pork chops (about 150-200 grams each)

100 grams all-purpose flour

2 large eggs

150 grams breadcrumbs

Salt and pepper to taste

Vegetable oil for frying

For the burger:

4 burger buns

lettuce leaves

sliced tomatoes

sliced onions

sliced pickles

your desired sauce

tools

frying pan
