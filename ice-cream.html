Banana Ice-Cream

Enjoy Vegan Banana Ice Cream: a guilt-free delight made from frozen bananas blended into creamy perfection. It's a refreshing dairy-free treat, easily customizable with your favorite toppings.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Dessert Vegan

instructions

step 1

Slice the ripe bananas into coins and place them in a single layer on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Freeze the bananas for at least 2 hours or until completely frozen.

step 2

Once the bananas are frozen, transfer them to a food processor or high-speed blender. Add the creamy peanut butter or almond butter (if using) and maple syrup or agave nectar (if using).

step 3

Process the ingredients until smooth and creamy, stopping to scrape down the sides of the bowl or blender as needed. This may take a few minutes, and you may need to pause and stir the mixture occasionally to help it blend evenly.

step 4

Taste the banana ice cream and adjust the sweetness or flavorings to your liking. Add more maple syrup/agave or peanut butter/almond butter if desired.

step 5

Once blended to your desired consistency, scoop the banana ice cream into bowls and serve immediately.

step 6

Garnish the banana ice cream with your favorite toppings, such as chopped nuts, vegan chocolate chips, or shredded coconut.

ingredients

3 ripe bananas, peeled, sliced, and frozen

2 tablespoons of creamy peanut butter or almond butter

1-2 tablespoons of maple syrup or agave nectar

Optional toppings: chopped nuts, vegan chocolate chips, shredded coconut

1 large egg

tools

food processor
