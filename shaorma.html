Shawarma

Shawarma: A flavorful Middle Eastern delight, featuring thinly sliced, marinated meat roasted to perfection on a vertical spit. Served in warm pita or wrapped in flatbread, it's accompanied by fresh veggies, pickles, and creamy sauces for a satisfying burst of taste in every bite.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Meat Dishes

instructions

step 1

Marinate Chicken: Combine chicken with minced garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, spices, salt, and pepper. Refrigerate for 30 minutes or overnight.

step 2

Cook Chicken: Grill or roast marinated chicken until cooked through and slightly charred.

step 3

Prepare Accompaniments: Slice vegetables and warm bread. Prepare sauce.

step 4

Assemble Shawarma: Place cooked chicken on bread, add vegetables, sauce, and pickles if desired.

step 5

Serve: Roll up shawarma tightly and serve warm.

ingredients

For the Marinade:

500 grams of chicken breast or thigh fillets, thinly sliced

4 cloves of garlic, minced

2 tablespoons of lemon juice

2 tablespoons of olive oil

1 teaspoon of ground cumin

1 teaspoon of paprika

1/2 teaspoon of ground turmeric

1/2 teaspoon of ground coriander

1/2 teaspoon of ground cinnamon

Salt and black pepper to taste

For serving:

pita bread or flatbread

sliced tomatoes

sliced cucumbers

sliced onions

garlic sauce

tomato sauce

sweet corn

tools

grill
