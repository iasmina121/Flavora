Pancakes

Wake up to the perfect breakfast with our fluffy golden pancakes! Light, airy, and cooked to perfection, these pancakes are a delightful treat any time of day. Top with your favorite toppings for a customizable taste experience that's sure to satisfy. Enjoy the simple pleasure of homemade goodness today!

prep time

15 min.

cook time

20 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Dessert

instructions

step 1

Mix Dry Ingredients: In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the all-purpose flour, granulated sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt until well combined.

step 2

Prepare Wet Ingredients: In a separate bowl, beat the egg and then add the buttermilk (or milk mixed with lemon juice or vinegar), melted butter, and vanilla extract. Mix until everything is well combined.

step 3

Combine Wet and Dry Ingredients: Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry ingredients. Stir gently until just combined, being careful not to overmix. It's okay if the batter is a bit lumpy.

step 4

Preheat Griddle or Pan: Place a non-stick griddle or large skillet over medium heat. If using a skillet, add a little butter or oil to grease the surface.

step 5

Cook Pancakes: Once the griddle or skillet is hot, pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the surface for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancakes and the edges begin to look set, about 2-3 minutes.

step 6

Flip and Cook: Carefully flip each pancake with a spatula and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes on the other side, until golden brown and cooked through.

step 7

Serve: Transfer the cooked pancakes to a plate and serve warm with your favorite toppings, such as maple syrup, fresh berries, or whipped cream.

ingredients

120g flour

2 tablespoons sugar

1 teaspoon baking powder

1/2 teaspoon baking soda

a pinch of salt

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

240ml milk

30g butter melted

tools

Whisk or electric mixer
