Avocado toast

Avocado toast is a delicious breakfast option made by spreading mashed avocado on toasted bread. It's easy to make, customizable with toppings like eggs or tomatoes, and packed with flavor. Try it and see why it's become a go-to choice for breakfast lovers everywhere!

prep time

5 min.

cook time

2 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : vegan

instructions

step 1

Start by toasting the bread to your desired level of crispiness.

step 2

While the bread is toasting, slice open your ripe avocados and scoop the flesh into a bowl.

step 3

Mash the avocado with a fork until it reaches your preferred consistency.

step 4

Season the mashed avocado with a pinch of salt and pepper to taste, and mix well.

step 5

Once the bread is toasted, spread the mashed avocado evenly onto each slice.

step 6

If desired, add any optional toppings such as sliced tomatoes, a poached or fried egg, red pepper flakes, or sesame seeds.

step 7

Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade avocado toast!

ingredients

Ripe avocados

Bread

Salt

Pepper

tools

Fork
