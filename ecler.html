Eclairs

Eclairs are delicious French pastries made from light, airy dough filled with creamy goodness like vanilla or chocolate cream, and topped with a shiny chocolate glaze. They're a delightful treat with a perfect balance of textures and flavors, enjoyed by pastry lovers around the world.

prep time

45 min.

cook time

45 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Dessert

instructions

step 1

In a saucepan, combine water, milk, butter, salt, and sugar (if using). Bring to a boil over medium heat. Add flour all at once, stirring vigorously until a smooth dough forms. Continue to cook and stir for about 2 minutes until the dough pulls away from the sides of the pan and forms a ball. Transfer the dough to a mixing bowl and let it cool slightly. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, mixing well after each addition, until the dough is smooth and glossy.

step 2

Preheat the oven to 200Â°C . Line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Transfer the choux pastry dough into a piping bag fitted with a large round tip. Pipe the dough onto the prepared baking sheet into 10-12 centimeter lengths, leaving space between each eclair. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the eclairs are puffed up and golden brown. Remove from the oven and let them cool on a wire rack.

step 3

In a saucepan, heat the milk until it just begins to simmer. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg yolks, sugar, and cornstarch until smooth. Gradually pour the hot milk into the egg mixture, whisking constantly to temper the eggs. Return the mixture to the saucepan and cook over medium heat, stirring constantly, until thickened. Remove from heat, stir in the vanilla extract, and transfer the pastry cream to a bowl. Cover with plastic wrap, pressing it directly onto the surface of the cream to prevent a skin from forming. Chill in the refrigerator until ready to use.

step 4

Once the eclairs have cooled completely, use a sharp knife to make a small slit on the side of each eclair. Transfer the chilled pastry cream to a piping bag fitted with a small round tip. Pipe the cream into each eclair through the slit until they are filled.

step 5

In a heatproof bowl, combine the dark chocolate, heavy cream, and butter. Place the bowl over a pot of simmering water (double boiler) and stir until melted and smooth. Remove from heat and stir in the powdered sugar (if using). Let the glaze cool slightly.

step 6

Dip the top of each filled eclair into the chocolate glaze, allowing any excess to drip off. Place the glazed eclairs on a wire rack to set for a few minutes.

step 7

Once the glaze has set, your eclairs are ready to be served! Enjoy these delicious pastries as a delightful treat with a cup of coffee or tea.

ingredients

For the choux pastry:

125g of all-purpose flour

100ml of water

100ml of whole milk

80g of unsalted butter

4 large eggs

A pinch of salt

1 tablespoon of sugar (optional)

For the pastry cream filling:

500ml whole milk

4 large egg yolks

100g granulated sugar

40g cornstarch

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

For the chocolate glaze:

100g dark chocolate

50ml heavy cream

20g unsalted butter

2 tablespoons of powdered sugar (optional)

tools

Whisk or electric mixer
