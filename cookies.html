Chocolate Chip Cookies

Savor the classic comfort of chocolate chip cookies. Our irresistible recipe features buttery dough packed with gooey chocolate chips. Crisp edges give way to a soft, chewy center, ensuring each bite is pure delight. Perfect with a glass of milk or as a standalone treat, these cookies are a timeless favorite for all.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Dessert

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

step 2

In a medium-sized bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.

step 3

In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until light and fluffy.

step 4

Beat in the egg and vanilla extract until well combined.

step 5

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.

step 6

Gently fold in the chocolate chips until evenly distributed throughout the dough.

step 7

Using a spoon or cookie scoop, portion out the dough into balls and place them on the prepared baking sheet, leaving some space between each cookie.

step 8

Bake in the preheated oven for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are golden brown.

step 9

Allow the cookies to cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.

step 10

Enjoy your homemade chocolate chip cookies with a glass of milk or your favorite hot beverage!

ingredients

250g flour

150g unsalted butter, softened

80g granulated sugar

80g brown sugar

1 large egg

1/2 teaspoons baking powder

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

200g chocolate chips

Pinch of salt

tools

whisk
