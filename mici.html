Mici

Mici: Romania's savory sensation! These delicious grilled minced meat rolls, bursting with flavor and spices, are a beloved staple of Romanian cuisine. Perfect for any occasion, these juicy morsels are sure to delight your taste buds and transport you to the vibrant streets of Romania with every bite!

prep time

20 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Meat Dishes Romanian Cuisine

instructions

step 1

Prepare the Meat Mixture: In a large mixing bowl, combine the ground beef and ground pork. Add the finely chopped onion, minced garlic, sweet paprika, ground cumin, dried thyme, dried oregano, dried marjoram, ground black pepper, salt, and baking soda to the meat mixture.

step 2

Mix the Ingredients: Using clean hands or a spoon, thoroughly mix all the ingredients until well combined. Ensure that the spices are evenly distributed throughout the meat mixture.

step 3

Add Water: Gradually add 2-3 tablespoons of cold water to the meat mixture. This helps to moisten the mixture and makes it easier to shape into rolls.

step 4

Shape the Mici: Take a handful of the meat mixture and shape it into small cylindrical rolls, about 6-7cm in length and 2cm in diameter. Press the meat firmly to ensure the rolls hold their shape during grilling.

step 5

Grill the Mici: Preheat a grill or barbecue to medium-high heat. Place the shaped mici onto the grill grate and cook for about 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally, until they are evenly browned and cooked through.

step 6

Serve: Once the mici are cooked, remove them from the grill and let them rest for a few minutes. Serve the Romanian mici hot with your favorite accompaniments, such as mustard, ketchup, fresh bread, or pickles.

ingredients

500 grams of ground beef

300 grams of ground pork

1 medium onion, finely chopped

3 cloves of garlic, minced

1 teaspoon of sweet paprika

1 teaspoon of ground cumin

1 teaspoon of dried thyme

1 teaspoon of dried oregano

1 teaspoon of dried marjoram

1 teaspoon of ground black pepper

1 teaspoon of salt

1/4 teaspoon of baking soda

2-3 tablespoons of cold water

tools

grill or barbecue
