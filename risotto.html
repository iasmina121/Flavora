Mushroom Risotto

Risotto: A creamy Italian delight, where Arborio rice meets a flavorful broth, resulting in a velvety texture. Enhanced with butter, garlic, and Parmesan, it's a versatile dish, perfect as a main or side, promising a symphony of flavors in every bite.

prep time

10 min.

cook time

25 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Prepare Ingredients: Heat the broth in a saucepan and keep it warm over low heat. Clean and slice the mushrooms, chop the onion, mince the garlic, and grate the Parmesan cheese.

step 2

Sauté Mushrooms: In a large skillet or pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the sliced mushrooms and sauté until they are golden brown and tender. Remove them from the pan and set aside.

step 3

Cook Onions and Garlic: In the same skillet, melt 15 grams of butter. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until they are soft and translucent.

step 4

Toast Rice: Add the Arborio rice to the skillet with the onions and garlic. Stir the rice for about 1-2 minutes until it becomes slightly translucent around the edges.

step 5

Deglaze with Wine (optional): If using white wine, pour it into the skillet and stir until it is mostly absorbed by the rice.

step 6

Add Broth: Begin adding the warm broth to the skillet, one ladleful at a time, stirring frequently. Allow the rice to absorb the broth before adding more. Continue this process until the rice is creamy and al dente, which usually takes about 18-20 minutes.

step 7

Incorporate Mushrooms: About halfway through cooking the rice, return the sautéed mushrooms to the skillet, allowing them to heat through with the risotto.

step 8

Finish with Butter and Parmesan: Once the rice is cooked to your desired consistency, remove the skillet from the heat. Stir in the remaining 15 grams of butter and grated Parmesan cheese until melted and creamy. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

step 9

Serve: Serve the mushroom risotto hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired. Enjoy the creamy, flavorful goodness of this classic Italian dish!

ingredients

200g Araborio rice

1 liter of chicken or vegetable broth (heated)

250g mushrooms (such as cremini or porcini), sliced

1 small onion, finely chopped

2 cloves of garlic, minced

30g butter

15ml olive oil

120ml white wine (optional)

50g parmesan cheese, grated

salt, to taste

black pepper, to taste

fresh parlsey, for granish (optional)

tools

pan
