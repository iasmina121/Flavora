Romanian "chec"

Romanian "chec" is a beloved sponge cake enjoyed for its simplicity and comforting taste. Made with basic ingredients like flour, sugar, eggs, and oil or butter, it can be customized with flavors like cocoa powder or lemon zest. Light and fluffy, it's perfect for any occasion, embodying the warmth of Romanian hospitality.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Dessert Romanian Cuisine

instructions

step 1

Preheat your oven to 180Â°C. Grease and flour a loaf pan or line it with parchment paper for easy removal.

step 2

In a large mixing bowl, beat together the eggs and sugar until pale and fluffy.

step 3

Gradually add the vegetable oil or melted butter to the egg mixture, mixing well until fully incorporated. If using, add vanilla extract and lemon zest for additional flavor.

step 4

In a separate bowl, sift together the flour and baking powder.

step 5

Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined. Be careful not to overmix, as this can result in a dense cake.

step 6

If making a chocolate variation, divide the batter in half and mix cocoa powder into one portion until well combined.

step 7

Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan, spreading it evenly.

step 8

Bake in the preheated oven for 40-50 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

step 9

Once baked, remove the cake from the oven and let it cool in the pan for about 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.

step 10

Slice and serve your Romanian "chec" plain or with your favorite toppings, such as powdered sugar or whipped cream.

ingredients

250 g flour

150 g sugar

4 eggs

120 ml oil

2 teaspoons baking powder

1 teaspoon vanilla extract

zest of 1 lemon (optional)

2 tablespoons of cocoa powder

tools

Whisk or electric mixer
