Sarmale

Sarmale are Romanian cabbage rolls stuffed with a mix of minced meat, rice, onions, and spices. They're simmered in a tomato broth until tender and served hot, often with sour cream or polenta. It's a classic comfort food, enjoyed during holidays and year-round.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

2h.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Romanian Cuisine Meat Dishes

instructions

step 1

Prepare the Cabbage Leaves: If using fresh cabbage, remove the core and carefully separate the leaves. Blanch them in boiling water for a few minutes to soften, then drain and set aside. If using pickled cabbage leaves, rinse them gently under cold water to remove excess saltiness.

step 2

Prepare the Filling: In a large mixing bowl, combine the minced meat, rice, finely chopped onion, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, paprika, and dried thyme. Mix well until evenly combined.

step 3

Roll the Sarmale: Place a cabbage leaf flat on a clean surface. Add a spoonful of the meat and rice mixture onto the center of the leaf. Fold the sides of the leaf over the filling, then roll it up tightly to form a neat parcel. Repeat with the remaining cabbage leaves and filling mixture.

step 4

Layer in a Pot: Line the bottom of a large pot with sauerkraut or sour cabbage leaves. Arrange the rolled cabbage parcels in the pot, seam side down, in a single layer.

step 5

Add Tomato Sauce and Water: Pour the crushed tomatoes over the rolled cabbage parcels. Then, pour the water over them, ensuring they are partially submerged.

step 6

Simmer: Cover the pot and bring the liquid to a gentle simmer over medium-low heat. Let the sarmale cook for 1.5 to 2.5 hours, or until the cabbage leaves are tender and the filling is cooked through. Check occasionally and add more broth if needed to prevent sticking or drying out.

step 7

Serve: Once cooked, remove the sarmale from the pot and serve them hot. Optionally, garnish with fresh chopped dill or parsley, and serve with sour cream and polenta or mamaliga on the side.

ingredients

1 large cabbage head

500g minced meat (pork, beef or a combination)

200g rice

1 large onion, finely chopped

2-3 cloves or garlic, minced

1 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon black pepper

1 teaspoon paprika

1 teaspoon dried thyme

400g crushed tomatoes

500ml water

Sour cream, for serving (optional)

Polenta or mamaliga, for serving (optional)

Fresh dill or parsley, chopped, for garnish (optional)

tools

large pot
