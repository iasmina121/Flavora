Falafel

Falafel is a popular Middle Eastern dish made from ground chickpeas or fava beans, mixed with herbs, spices, and onions. The mixture is formed into small balls or patties and deep-fried until golden and crispy on the outside, while remaining soft and flavorful on the inside. Falafel is often served in pita bread with salad, tahini sauce, and other condiments, making it a delicious and satisfying vegetarian or vegan option enjoyed worldwide.

prep time

20 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : vegan

instructions

step 1

Place the dried chickpeas in a large bowl and cover with water. Let them soak overnight or for at least 8 hours. Drain and rinse well before using.

step 2

In a food processor, combine the soaked and drained chickpeas, chopped onion, minced garlic, parsley, coriander, ground cumin, ground coriander, baking powder, salt, and pepper. Pulse until the mixture is finely ground but not pureed. You may need to scrape down the sides of the processor bowl occasionally.

step 3

Transfer the falafel mixture to a bowl. With slightly damp hands, shape the mixture into small balls or patties, about 1 1/2 inches in diameter.

step 4

In a large skillet or frying pan, heat vegetable oil over medium-high heat until hot but not smoking. Carefully add the falafel balls or patties to the hot oil, being careful not to overcrowd the pan. Fry the falafel in batches, turning occasionally, until they are golden brown and crispy on all sides, about 3-4 minutes per side. Adjust the heat as needed to maintain a steady frying temperature. Once cooked, remove the falafel from the oil with a slotted spoon and transfer them to a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.

step 5

Serve the falafel warm, either on their own as a snack or appetizer, or stuffed into pita bread with salad greens and your choice of sauce (such as tahini or yogurt sauce). Add pickles or olives on the side for extra flavor, if desired.

step 6

Enjoy your homemade falafel as a delicious and satisfying meal or snack!

ingredients

250 grams of dried chickpeas

1 small onion, roughly chopped

3 cloves of garlic, minced

1 handful of fresh parsley, chopped

1 handful of fresh coriander (cilantro), chopped

1 teaspoon of ground cumin

1 teaspoon of ground coriander

1/2 teaspoon of baking powder

1 teaspoon of dried marjoram

Salt and pepper to taste

Vegetable oil for frying

tools

food processor or blender
