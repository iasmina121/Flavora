Romanian "Ciorba de pui à la grecque"

"Ciorba de pui à la grecque" is a Greek-style chicken soup featuring tender chicken, vegetables, and fragrant herbs in a flavorful broth with a touch of lemon. Comforting and refreshing, it's perfect for any mealtime craving or chilly day.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

30 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Romanian Cuisine Meat Dishes

instructions

step 1

In a large pot, heat a drizzle of olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until fragrant.

step 2

Add the diced chicken breast to the pot and cook until browned on all sides.

step 3

Stir in the diced carrots and celery, cook for a few minutes until slightly softened.

step 4

Pour in enough water to cover the ingredients in the pot. Add the bay leaf and season with salt and pepper to taste.

step 5

Bring the soup to a simmer and let it cook for about 20-25 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender and the chicken is cooked through.

step 6

Once the soup is ready, remove it from the heat and stir in the lemon juice.

step 8

Serve the ciorba de pui à la grecque hot, garnished with freshly chopped parsley. Enjoy!

step 7

Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.

ingredients

500g chicken breast, diced

2 carrots, diced

1 onion, chopped

2 celery stalks, chopped

2 cloves garlic, minced

Juice of 1 lemon

Salt and pepper to taste

Fresh parsley for garnish

Olive oil for cooking

tools

large pot
