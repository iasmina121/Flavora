Greek bowl

Introducing the Greek Bowl: a delicious combination of oven-roasted chicken, crispy potatoes, and creamy tzatziki sauce. Whether for a summer BBQ or a quick dinner, this bowl brings the flavors of Greece to your table. With a side of fresh Greek salad, it's a satisfying and easy meal you'll love!

prep time

20 min.

cook time

40 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Meat Dishes

instructions

step 1

Marinate chicken in olive oil, garlic, herbs, salt, and pepper. Refrigerate.

step 2

Toss potatoes with olive oil, herbs, salt, and pepper. Roast in preheated oven.

step 3

Make tzatziki sauce by combining Greek yogurt, cucumber, garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, dill, salt, and pepper.

step 4

Prepare Greek salad with cucumbers, red onion, feta cheese, lemon juice, olive oil, herbs, salt, and pepper.

step 5

Cook marinated chicken on a grill pan until golden and cooked through.

step 6

Assemble bowls with chicken, potatoes, tzatziki sauce, and Greek salad.

ingredients

For the chicken:

680 g boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into small pieces

2 tablespoons olive oil

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1 teaspoons paprika

1 teaspoons dried thyme

salt and pepper to taste

For the oven roasted potatoes:

900g potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 teaspoon dried oregano

1 teaspoon dried rosemary

Salt and pepper to taste

For the tzatziki sauce:

240g greek yogurt

1/2 cucumber, grated and excess water squeezed out

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice

1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped

salt and pepper to taste

For the greek salad:

2 cucumbers, diced

1/2 red onion, thinly sliced

100 g crumbled feta chesse

2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

1 tablespoon of fresh marjoram, chopped

salt and pepper to taste

tools

grill or grill pan
