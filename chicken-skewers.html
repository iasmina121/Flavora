Chicken skewers

Chicken skewers are a delicious dish featuring tender chicken pieces marinated in a flavorful blend of spices and grilled to perfection. They're perfect for summer barbecues or as a quick and easy weeknight meal.

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

Tags : Meat Dishes

instructions

step 1

In a mixing bowl, combine the olive oil, minced garlic, lemon juice, paprika, oregano, salt, and black pepper. Mix well to combine.

step 2

Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, making sure they are well coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

step 3

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.

step 4

Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers, alternating with the onion, bell peppers, and cherry tomatoes.

step 5

Grill the skewers for about 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the chicken is cooked through and the vegetables are tender and slightly charred.

step 6

Serve the chicken skewers hot off the grill with your favorite sides, such as rice, grilled bread, or tzatziki sauce.

ingredients

680 g boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces

60 ml olive oil

3 cloves garlic, minced

2 tablespoons lemon juice

1 teaspoons paprika

1 teaspoon salt

1/2 teaspoon black pepper

1 red onion, cut into bite-sized pieces

1/2 teaspoon black pepper

1 red onion, cut into bite-sized pieces

1 red bell pepper, cut into bite-sized pieces

150g cherry tomatoes

tools

grill or grill pan
